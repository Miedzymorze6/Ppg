KonigLabs

Word hybridization algorithm app (Dutch - German)

This program with unique algorithm calculates the similarity between a Dutch word and a German word. It determines their initial binary similarity and generates hybrid words by blending characters based on their calculated similarity scores. Up to 50 hybrid words are generated and evaluated for their resemblance to both original words using SequenceMatcher. Words with a similarity score below 0.5 are excluded, and the top results are sorted and displayed.
